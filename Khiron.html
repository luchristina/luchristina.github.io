Khiron

An investor relations concept for a medicinal cannabis corporation


I. Project overview

I worked with Equisolve to come up with a concept for the investor relations page of Khiron, a medicinal cannabis company. I was given 3 days to come up with a concept to provide to the creative director of Equisolve. The task was to take the content strategy document and conceptualize the information into an appealing and easy-to-understand layout.

II. Limitations

Time limit Due to working full-time during the day, I was limited with the hours I could work on this project. I spent 3.25 hours on Monday, and 8 hours on Tuesday. The total amount of time spent on this concept was 11.25 hours.

III. Design

The content strategy was already prepared and the client has a logo that includes the brand colors. First, I designed a wireframe to give me an idea of the placement of images and text. Next, I moved onto creating the mockup that I eventually sent to the creative director for review.

IV. Review

Due to the time limitation for this concept, I feel that there were areas on the page where I could experiment with the arrangement of information. Additionally, I would add extra space above the “Download presentation” and between the information that follows. The overlapping of images provides a nice transition into the next piece of text.

Overall I am happy with the product I created given the time limitation.
