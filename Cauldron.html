Cauldron

A grocery and recipe app aimed to help shoppers save time at the grocery store


View Prototype

I. About the project

Role UX/UI Designer
Duration 1 month (May 2019-Jun 2019)
Tools Figma, UsabilityHub, Maze, GSuite

The problem Grocery shopping is a task that many people do regularly. When shopping, people might walk back and forth between aisles to grab items they have forgotten. This wasted time adds up when grocery shopping is a reoccuring task.


The goal The aim is to cut down the time shoppers spend at the grocery store. I believe that creating a well designed recipe and grocery app will solve this issue.

II. Research

I deployed a survey to various individuals. Of the responses I received, these were the main correlations.



  • The biggest complaints were due to time spent.
  • 73% of this sample stated that they use a physical or digital notepad.

    VIII. Key Findings

    Overall Designing the right layout was important to the usability of the app. The recipe list and detail screens' layouts changed dramtically over each iteration with the goal of looking orderly and easy-to-use.


    What I would've done differently Keeping accessibility in mind from the beginning is very important and can save a lot of time during screen updates. I would also spend additional time brainstorming different layout ideas in order to minimize the changes needed for each iteration.


    If I had more time I would deploy this product and measure the amount of time a user saves with this app. Getting these numbers would be helpful for future testing as a measure of success.
