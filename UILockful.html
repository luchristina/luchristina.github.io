Lockful

A home page created for a product that provides access to all social media sites with one login

I. Intro

The UI design for this product's home page caters to social influencers and their content management team. Whether it is for a small or large team, managers are able to select their appropriate plan that works for their client size.

Duration 1 day

II. Features

  • I added a 'recommended' plan and utilized color and hierarchy to have it stand out.
  • Describing the product and its competencies is important when selling a product. The features listed could entice the reader to subscribe.
  • Need to ask questions? The footer of the home page provides phone number, email, and social handles to reach out.

III. Color Palette



The colors I chose were contrasted where it was neccessary and soft for the rest. The peach palette represents the idea of being genuine and sincere, which is important for a user to feel when entrusting their data with this security/social media product.
