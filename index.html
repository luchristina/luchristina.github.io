My path to design

It started at the age of 13. I was fascinated by the various themes on social media sites. Not long after, I taught myself how to graphic design and code by diving into forums, websites, and source codes.

Even though I graduated with a degree in Finance, there are transferable skills between these two career fields. For example, it is crucial to understand each client's needs in both professions. Other transferrable skills include crossfunctional communication and data analysis.

Outside of the UX world, I enjoy visiting Disney parks, going on food adventures, and getting cuddles from my 28 lb goldendoodle.
