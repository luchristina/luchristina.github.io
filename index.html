My path to design

It started at the age of 13. I was fascinated by the way people would customize their profiles on social media sites, so I took the initiative to learn how to do it myself by diving into forums, websites, and source codes. This led me to start my college career majoring in Computer Science; my school did not have a web development major. It wasn't long until I realized that I wasn't a fan of back-end coding, so I changed my major into Finance, a safe alternative career field to dive into.

Even though I graduated with a degree in Finance, there are transferable skills between these different career fields. For example, it was crucial to understand each client's needs. I'd also communicate with internal and external teams on a daily basis to ensure tasks are being completed.
