My path to design

It began at the age of 13. I was fascinated by the various site themes on social media. Not long after, I dove into design forums, exploring websites, and source codes in order to teach myself graphic design and how to code.

My background in Finance has given me many tools to use in the realm of design. I am skilled in crossfunctional communication, data analysis, and have a knack for understanding the needs of the client.

Outside of the UX world, I enjoy visiting Disney parks, going on food adventures, and hanging out with my goldendoodle.

Currently reading The Design of Everyday Things by Don Norman.
